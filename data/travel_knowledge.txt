USA - New York:
New York City is known for Times Square, Central Park, Statue of Liberty, Empire State Building, Broadway shows, museums, and skyline views. Popular activities include city tours, shopping in Manhattan, food tours, ferry rides, and nightlife. Budget travelers prefer public transport, Central Park visits, and street food, while luxury travelers enjoy rooftop dining and premium hotels.

USA - Miami:
Miami is known for beaches, nightlife, art districts, Cuban food, shopping, and water activities. Popular places include South Beach, Little Havana, Wynwood Walls, Bayside Marketplace, and nearby cruise trips. Visitors enjoy beach relaxation, nightlife, water sports, and cultural tours. It is popular for both budget beach trips and luxury resort stays.

USA - Las Vegas:
Las Vegas is famous for casinos, nightlife, luxury hotels, themed resorts, live shows, shopping, and entertainment. Popular attractions include the Las Vegas Strip, Bellagio fountains, Fremont Street, and nearby Hoover Dam. Visitors enjoy shows, gaming, nightlife, and luxury experiences. It is also a gateway to the Grand Canyon.

USA - Los Angeles:
Los Angeles is known for Hollywood, Universal Studios, Santa Monica Pier, Beverly Hills, beaches, and film culture. Popular activities include studio tours, beach visits, shopping, sightseeing, and theme parks. Travelers enjoy a mix of entertainment, relaxation, and celebrity culture.

USA - San Francisco:
San Francisco is known for the Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz Island, Fisherman’s Wharf, cable cars, and waterfront views. Popular activities include city tours, bay cruises, visiting museums, and exploring nearby tech hubs. It is ideal for scenic, cultural, and relaxed travel experiences.

USA - Chicago:
Chicago is known for Millennium Park, architecture tours, museums, shopping, and lakefront attractions. Visitors enjoy deep-dish pizza, riverwalk tours, skyline views, and cultural experiences. It is great for both urban exploration and food tourism.

USA - Orlando:
Orlando is famous for theme parks like Walt Disney World, Universal Studios, and family entertainment resorts. It is ideal for family vacations, offering amusement parks, water parks, and kid-friendly attractions.

USA - Washington DC:
Washington DC is known for monuments, museums, government buildings, and historical sites. Popular attractions include the White House, Lincoln Memorial, Capitol Building, and Smithsonian museums. It is ideal for educational and cultural travel.

India - Goa:
Goa is known for beaches, nightlife, forts, churches, water sports, seafood, and local markets. North Goa includes Baga Beach, Calangute Beach, Anjuna Beach, Fort Aguada, beach shacks, nightlife, and shopping areas. South Goa includes Palolem Beach, Colva Beach, peaceful resorts, scenic beaches, and relaxed sightseeing. Visitors enjoy beach activities, nightlife, shopping, and water sports.

India - Hyderabad:
Hyderabad is known for Charminar, Golconda Fort, biryani, shopping markets, and historical landmarks. Popular activities include heritage tours, food exploration, and shopping in local bazaars.

India - Bangalore:
Bangalore is known for parks, cafes, nightlife, tech hubs, and shopping areas. Popular activities include cafe hopping, nightlife experiences, and nearby weekend getaways.

India - Mumbai:
Mumbai is known for Marine Drive, Gateway of India, Bollywood, street food, shopping, nightlife, and coastal attractions. Visitors enjoy city tours, food exploration, and cultural experiences.

India - Delhi:
Delhi is known for historical monuments, markets, street food, and cultural heritage. Popular places include Red Fort, India Gate, Qutub Minar, and Chandni Chowk.

India - Jaipur:
Jaipur is known for forts, palaces, heritage sites, and cultural tourism. Popular attractions include Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal, and local markets.

India - Kerala:
Kerala is known for backwaters, houseboats, greenery, beaches, and Ayurveda. Visitors enjoy nature, relaxation, and scenic travel experiences.

Travel Styles:
Budget travel includes public transport, local food, basic hotels, and free attractions.
Luxury travel includes premium hotels, fine dining, private tours, and exclusive experiences.
Family travel includes theme parks, kid-friendly attractions, safe accommodations, and planned activities.
Adventure travel includes trekking, water sports, exploration, and outdoor activities.

General Travel Tips:
Best time to travel depends on destination and weather.
Always consider budget, safety, and local transport options.
Plan activities based on interests like adventure, relaxation, or cultural exploration.
